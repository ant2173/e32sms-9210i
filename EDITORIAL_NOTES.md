# Editorial notes for the bilingual edition

## English edition

The English texts are adaptations, not literal translations. They use neutral international technical English, preserve the first-person voice and keep the narrative pacing of the Russian originals.

Terminology has been standardised around:

- `writable static data`;
- `window server`;
- `framebuffer`;
- `focus-gated drawing`;
- `hardware-tested` / `tested on original hardware`;
- `AI-assisted development`.

The awkward draft filename `02-boots-is-not-plays.md` has been replaced in both language folders with `02-booting-is-not-playing.md`. Part 1 has likewise been normalised to `01-hardcore-vibe-coding.md`.
